Poems

Whispers of the Wind

In the stillness of the night,
The whispers of the wind take flight,
Telling tales of far and wide,
Of dreams that in the heart reside.

Through the trees and over hills,
The wind carries secrets and thrills,
A gentle touch, a soothing song,
A companion as we journey along.

Echoes of the Past

In the echoes of the past,
We find memories that last,
Shadows of what used to be,
Reflections of our history.

Time moves on, yet we remain,
Tied to moments of joy and pain,
The past a mirror to our soul,
A part of us that makes us whole.

A Dance with the Stars

Beneath the vast and starry skies,
We dance where dreams and hope arise,
Each twinkle a promise, a beacon bright,
Guiding us through the darkest night.

Hand in hand, we twirl and sway,
Lost in the magic of the Milky Way,
A cosmic waltz, a timeless embrace,
In the starlight, we find our place.
